About Hyper Text SMS Bomber

The Hyper Text SMS Bomber is a powerful tool that allows you to send multiple SMS messages to a specific phone number. It can be used for various purposes, such as testing the resilience of SMS infrastructure or pranks among friends.

How to Use

  1. Click the link above to access the SMS Bomber.
  2. Enter the target phone number.
  3. Choose the number of messages you want to send.
  4. Click the "Send" button to initiate the SMS bombing process.
